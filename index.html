About me

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the Friedrich Miescher Institute in Computational Neuroscience in the team of Friedemann Zenke. Previously a PhD candidate in hardware-oriented AI at the Center for Nanoscience and Nanotechnology, attached to Paris-Saclay University, under the supervision of Damien Querlioz and Liza Herrera-Diez.

Research interests

I work on biologically plausible deep learning to reduce the energy cost of current AI models. More specifically, I am interested in biologically plausible learning algorithms and continual learning.
